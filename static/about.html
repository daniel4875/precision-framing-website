ABOUT

At Precision Framing we are dedicated to providing a quality, professional and personal service, no matter how large or small. With 28 years experience in picture framing and as former head framer at Art & Frame in Yate Shopping Centre, Matt has a wide range of expertise in all areas of framing. As a mobile framer, samples are brought to you at your convenience where we can discuss the various framing options, and you can choose the frames and mount colours in situ, giving you confidence in making the right choice. All our visits are free and no obligation. Our framing is done to a high standard and full conservation standards can be met on request.

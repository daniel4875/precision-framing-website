At Precision Framing we are dedicated to providing a quality, professional and personal service, no matter how large or small. With 20 years experience in picture framing as former head framer at Art & Frame in Yate Shopping Centre, Matt has a wide range of expertise in all areas of framing. As a mobile framer, samples are brought to you at your convenience and where you can choose the frames and mount colours in situation and have confidence in making the right choice. All framing is framed to a high conservation standard. Our aim is to protect and conserve your item.
